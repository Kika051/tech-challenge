About the kingdom

It was a powerful Colchis (Kolkheti) kingdom, where the king was Aeëtes. The Argonauts arrived exactly there to take a Golden Fleece. Most of the territories mentioned in the legend are real and described correctly. Colchis was a very rich country. They owned a golden fleece. It was an Aries who had a golden wool and on this Fleece it was written how to get the gold through the sand of the river. The Kingdom of Colchis (Egrisi by Georgian sources) was a large kingdom of western Georgia. In ancient Greek mythology, Colchis is mentioned as the country of origin of Aeëtes and Medea. Medea was the daughter of King Aeëtes of Colchis, Georgia.
